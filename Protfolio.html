Candid Wedding Photography

A wedding is a series of special moments in one’s life. The moments of love, trust, happiness and celebrations. It is that time when someone starts a new life while the other one departs from her family. It is the moment of a lifetime. It is the moment to be cherished forever. With the changing trends in every sphere of life, wedding photography has also taken a new form. Unlike conventional photography, these days people look for Candid Photography.A kind of photography which is not done with prior arrangements, poses and special set ups is known as Candid Photography. It is one-of-a-kind and very much popular these days, especially, the candid photography for weddings.

Wedding Cinematography

Yes, we call it wedding cinematography and not wedding videography because we believe that weddings are once in a lifetime affairs and they need the same treatment and artists as our cinema does. They need that same setup, that same passion and that same effort to produce one of its kind imagery that can be enjoyed with near and dear ones for years to come.At Alapon Photography, we conceptualise and design our wedding films after a meticulous study of our lead characters .. “The Bride & The Groom”.Stunning cinematography, immersive sound designing, crisp editing and a professional direction give you an opportunity to experience your most precious moments again and again for years to come.

Depending on your budget, you can choose from a 3-5 minutes short trailer or a Wedding Movie or both.

ACCOMMODATION & TRAVELLING

It requires travelling around India and abroad for photographing weddings. So travel is an essential part of the wedding package. Since you are the best to know your location and venue we leave it on you to take care of our travel and staying. This gives you flexibility to choose a place as per your choice and also arrange our stay as you prefer. Our fee for travel varies depending on the location and time of your event. You’ll find our travel fees quite reasonable as we charge at face value, meaning that we don’t make anything extra from the travel.

In what case we do need an accommodation ?

If we are shooting outside of Kolkata , and we don’t have our car / transport available , it will be appreciated if the client can arrange our stay.In case we do hire/rent a car for our Transport , we will pack and leave right after event.
